Reference

Common mistakes, deliverables checklist, and quick reference card.

⊕Common Mistakes

Every one of these has caused a revision round in past productions.

MistakeWhy It's WrongFix
Modern footage for historical eventsCreates a visual mismatch the audience noticesSearch archival footage or use a period-appropriate still image
Boring clip when a better one existsA plain Baskin-Robbins clip was replaced because a more interesting one existedCompare options, pick the one with more visual energy
Stock footage when real footage exists"People in suits" adds nothingSearch "[person] interview", "[person] CNBC" first
Video titles instead of URLsSlows down the pipeline — clips need to be found instantlyAlways raw YouTube URLs with exact timestamps
Host on screen 10+ secondsFeels like a lecture, not a documentaryBreak up every 3-5 seconds with B-roll or images
Clips under 3 secondsFlash for 1-2 seconds and feel jarringMinimum 3 seconds per clip; extend with a still image if needed
Silently skipping linesGap gets discovered during editing, pipeline stallsFlag: [NO B-ROLL FOUND - need alternative]
Reusing same clip for different linesRepetition looks lazyEach line needs a unique visual
Company logo instead of real footageA logo is the weakest possible visualSearch for office footage, news coverage, conference clips
3+ still images in a rowVideo feels like a slideshowBreak with at least one video clip every 2 images
